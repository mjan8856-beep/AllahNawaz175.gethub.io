Common Questions

General FAQs.

What is your typical turnaround time?
For full product designs, I typically work in 2-4 week sprints depending on complexity. Landing pages or small feature audits usually take 5-7 business days.
Do you handle development?
I specialize in high-end design, but I provide production-ready code via Framer or Webflow for marketing sites, and clean annotated files for developer handoff on custom apps.
Can you work with existing design systems?
Yes. I frequently lead the evolution of existing Figma libraries, ensuring new features maintain brand consistency while reducing design debt.
