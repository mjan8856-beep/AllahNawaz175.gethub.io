Detailed Case Studies

Smarter Influencer Marketing with AI

The Problem

Marketing teams were spending 15+ hours weekly manually searching for influencers across multiple platforms, leading to missed opportunities and inefficient campaign launches.

Research & Discovery

  • Conducted 12 user interviews with marketing managers
  • Analyzed competitor platforms and identified gaps
  • Created user journey maps highlighting pain points
  • Discovered 73% of users wanted AI-powered recommendations

The Solution

Designed an AI-powered dashboard that aggregates influencer data, provides smart recommendations, and automates campaign tracking—all in one unified interface.

  • Smart search with AI-powered filters
  • Automated performance analytics
  • One-click campaign management
